[FONT=trebuchet ms]List of commands:
[SPOILER="essential commands "]
[FONT=Trebuchet MS]/in - create your profile for your game statistics (required to perform any other command)
/post-<section> <post name> {<thread content>} - create a new post, you can get 1-10 posts, likes and possibly dislike. You should also refer to the different sections of the forums, please refer to the post names spoiler. (can be performed once a day) // !Thread content can only be added for the sections that are not profile post!
/repost-<section> <post name> - repost an already existing post, you can get 2~8 posts, likes and dislike. Section is not necessary for reposts. (posts that are less than 3 days old cannot be reposted, can be performed once a day)
/post-pf <content> - create a profile post, the latest of which would be shown at the top of your profile ingame, you will gain 1 post, 0-10 likes, 0-3 dislikes. (1 day cooldown)
/reply-<section> <original thread title> <your reply> - reply to a post already made on a section. Read more about it's benefits in the 'Extra Contents' section of this post. (1 day cooldown)
/postfarm - farms 30-110 posts, can postfarm every day.[/FONT]
/daily - Grants you 75 points per day during a season
[/SPOILER]
[SPOILER="advanced commands"]
[FONT=Trebuchet MS]/like <post name> - increase the like count by 20% in the post/profile post, may perform this command every 12 hours. (cannot like your own post)
/dislike <post name> - Increase the dislike count by 15% in the post/profile post, may perform this command every 12 hours. (cannot dislike your own post)
/follow <username> - following others can gain them a boost to show your appreciation! You can also get gifts from following.
(Followers give 10% more likes and -10% dislike/person (dislike maxed to -90%)
/compare - compare yourself to your actual forum statistics or other players in the game!
/rank buy/upgrade <rank name> - buy a rank to have extra benefits! Please refer to the rank store for the benefits.
/gift give <username> - give a gift to the person and you'll get 1 gift in return! (1 week cooldown)
/move <post> <section> - move a post to a different section! This does not affect stats.
/default <section> - select a default section. (1 week cooldown)[/FONT]
[/SPOILER][/FONT]
[SPOILER="Guilds command"]
[FONT=trebuchet ms]/guild create <name> -> Create a guild with the corresponding name
/guild setting -> check the setting part for more info.
/guild promote <username> -> For Owner only, promotes a player to Co-Owner
/guild demote <username> -> For Owner only, demotes a Co-Owner
/guild invite <username> -> For invite-only guilds only, sends an invite to a player
/guild accept -> after receiving an invite, use this command to accept the command
/guild join <guild name> -> For open guilds only, joins a guild immediately
/guild leave -> leave your current guild.
/guild kick <username> -> kick a player from the guild, does not mean banning, player can rejoin the guild at any time
/guild ban <username> -> ban a player from ever joining the guild again, as well as kicking them out of the guild at the same time
/guild unban <username> -> unban a player from the guild’s ban list.
/guild contribute <upgrade ID> <posts/likes> -> contribute to a guild upgrade with either posts or likes, more details in the contributions tab
/guild prioritize <upgrade ID> -> sets an upgrade to prioritize to show it to your guildmates, all unused contributions will be added to the prioritized upgrade.
/guild allocate <upgrade ID> -> allocate any unused points into an upgrade
/guild color <color> - sets a color for the guild name

Settings Page:
Below are the setting for guilds, only guildmasters are able to change these info
/guild setting join <open/invite> -> set the guild to be open or invite only. The default is invite only.
/guild setting bio <short text> -> add a short text for your guild, think of it as a promotion
/guild setting changename <name> -> change the guild’s name
/guild setting transfer <username> -> transfer the guild’s ownership to someone else[/FONT]
[/SPOILER]

[SPOILER="Cap for /postfarm and /daily"]

0 posts -> max 1250 points
1 post -> max 2000 points.
2 posts -> max 2500 points.
3 posts -> max 5000 points
10 posts -> max 10000 points.
10+ posts -> no limit
